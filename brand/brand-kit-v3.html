04 Brand Voice & Tone

Honest & Direct

Delytteful Living speaks plainly and truthfully. No spiritual jargon, no empty hype. If a resource is hard, say it. If it requires commitment, say it. The audience respects honesty far more than polish.

Do

"This course will challenge you. Show up anyway."

Avoid

"An amazing transformational journey awaits!"

Warm but Grounded

The brand is warm — it genuinely cares about the people it serves. But that warmth is not sugary or saccharine. It is the warmth of someone who has sat with hard questions and found God faithful through them.

Do

"You are further than you were. Keep going."

Avoid

"You're doing SO amazing!! Keep shining! ✨✨"

Rooted in Scripture

Every product, every piece of copy, every campaign flows from Scripture. Not as decoration but as foundation. The anchor verse — Psalm 37:4 — is the actual thesis of the brand, lived out across four pillars.

Do

Let the verse drive the message naturally.

Avoid

Adding scripture as decoration at the end.

08 Store Setup Guides

Etsy

Best for: Low-content books, journals, printable planners
1

Create Your Account

Go to etsy.com → "Sell on Etsy" → "Open your Etsy shop." Use the email tied to your business. Choose United States as shop location for broader reach (or Kenya — both work).

2

Shop Name & Preferences

Shop name: DelyttefulLiving (no spaces). Language: English. Currency: USD for maximum international reach.

3

Brand Your Shop

Upload banner (3360×840px) and icon (500×500px) using Ember & Ink hex codes from this kit. Write your shop bio referencing all four pillars.

4

Create Your First Listing

Upload PDF. Set delivery to Digital Download. Use keyword-rich titles: "Worship Guitar Course — Beginner to Intermediate PDF." Add all 13 tags.

5

Payment & Billing

Connect Etsy Payments. Budget for $0.20 listing fee per item + 6.5% transaction fee in your pricing.

Etsy SEO Tips

  • Put your most important keywords in the FIRST 40 characters of your title
  • Use all 13 tags — mix broad and specific terms per pillar
  • Aim for 5-star reviews early — ask buyers in your download confirmation message
  • List at least 10 products — shops with more listings rank higher
  • Offer bundles (e.g. Faith + Music starter pack) to increase average order value

Payhip

Best for: Direct sales, higher margins, bundles & courses
1

Create Your Account

Go to payhip.com → "Get Started Free." No monthly fee — 5% per transaction only. Connect PayPal or Stripe for payouts.

2

Set Up Your Store Page

Go to Store → Edit Store. Add your logo and Ember & Ink hex codes. Store URL: payhip.com/DelyttefulLiving

3

Upload Your Products

Click Add Product → Digital Download. Upload PDF, set price, add cover image (1600×2560px). Write descriptions with bullet-point benefits, not just features.

4

Create Bundles

Use Add Product → Bundle to group related products. Example: "The Worship Musician Bundle" — guitar course + music theory + drum patterns for $37.

5

Enable Affiliates & Coupons

Offer 20–30% affiliate commission. Create launch coupons (e.g. DELYTTEFUL20) for your YouTube and social audiences.

Payhip Best Practices

  • Link directly to Payhip from YouTube — you keep 95% vs Etsy's ~93%
  • Use Payhip's email collection to build your mailing list from day one
  • The Worship Musician Bundle ($37) will be your highest-converting product
  • Set up a free lead magnet (chord chart PDF) to capture emails for the music audience
  • Use Payhip for all high-ticket products ($17+) — better margin than Etsy
